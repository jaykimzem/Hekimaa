About the Marathon

More Than a Race.
A Movement.

The Proposed Hekima University Molo Marathon is a flagship event organised in partnership with the Proposed Hekima University, Global Link Media, and key development partners.

The marathon will bring together elite athletes, corporate organisations, students, community leaders, and international partners in a professionally organised race that reflects the values of unity, service, peace, and human dignity.

Rooted in the Jesuit tradition of education and intellectual development, Hekima University seeks to advance science, innovation, and social progress while contributing meaningful solutions to society's most pressing challenges.

The marathon therefore represents more than sport — it is a platform where athletics, education, research, and community empowerment come together to create long-term impact.

Peace & Community Initiative

Running Towards Unity

Molo and Kuresoi North have historically experienced periods of post-election conflict. The Hekima University Molo Marathon aims to serve as a powerful peace-building platform, bringing together communities from diverse backgrounds under a shared purpose.

Through inclusive participation, youth engagement, and positive messaging, the marathon promotes unity, reconciliation, and national cohesion as the country approaches future elections.
