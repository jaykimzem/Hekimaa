Flag Off Series

The journey to race day is already in motion. Each activation builds the momentum towards Kenya's most impactful marathon.

The Phumulo Marathon doesn't begin on race day — it begins now. The Flag Off Series is a collection of activation events bringing together communities, sponsors, volunteers, and supporters in the lead-up to the main event on 8th August 2026. Each event is a step forward. Each activation builds the momentum. Join us on this journey.
07 MAY 2025
Nakuru

Nakuru Activation

The first official activation of the Flag Off Series. Community members and supporters will gather to learn about the Phumulo Marathon, what it stands for, and how to get involved. This is the spark that lights the fire.

08 MAY 2025
Molo

Molo Activation

The second activation event, now taking place in Molo — the heartbeat of the marathon. The community comes together to celebrate and build anticipation for the main race happening right here on 8th August 2026.

15 MAY 2025
Nairobi (Karura)

Youth Karura Activation

Energetic youth volunteers from a local parish gather in Nairobi to generate excitement and awareness around the marathon. This activation is youth-led, community-driven, and full of energy.
