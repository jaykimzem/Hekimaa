A Global Legacy

The Jesuit Tradition

The Proposed Hekima University follows in the footsteps of a 470-year-old Jesuit higher quality educational tradition. From Georgetown in the USA to Sophia University in Japan, Jesuit education is synonymous with rigorous inquiry, holistic human formation, and the pursuit of "Magis" — the more, the greater, the excellence.

Our pedagogy is rooted in the Ignatian Pedagogical Paradigm: Context, Experience, Reflection, Action, and Evaluation. We don't just teach students what to think, but how to think and why to act.

The Site

The Heart of Molo

Located in Molo, Nakuru County, the Proposed Hekima University campus will sit on 70 acres of pristine highland territory at 2,400m above sea level.

The campus is designed to be a model of sustainability and architectural integration, reflecting the natural beauty of the Kenyan highlands while providing state-of-the-art facilities for research and learning.
