The Society of Jesus

The Foundation

Hekima University College was founded in 1984 as a School of Theology by the Jesuit Conference of Africa and Madagascar. Today, we carry that legacy forward into the heart of Molo, establishing the Proposed Hekima University (PHU).

PHU is envisioned as an independent private Catholic university, rooted in Ignatian pedagogy and the Jesuit humanistic heritage, emphasizing excellence, rigor, and holistic formation.
