Join the Team

The Phumulo Marathon is more than a race — it is a community effort. We see your effort. We value your input. Together, we are building something that matters.

The Phumulo Marathon is more than a race — it is a community effort. Every marshal, every water station attendant, every registration staff member, and every crowd controller plays a role in making this event extraordinary.

Volunteers will receive an official certificate of participation that can be added to their professional portfolio and resume. This is your chance to be part of Kenya's most exciting marathon event and contribute to a cause that is changing lives through education.
